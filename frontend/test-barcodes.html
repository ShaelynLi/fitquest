Test Barcodes for Food Scanner

Display these on your computer screen and scan with your phone camera:

Barcode 1: Spanish Manzanilla Olives

Barcode: 012345678905

Barcode 2: Cheddar Cheese Curds

Barcode: 123456789012

Instructions:

  1. Open your food app on your phone
  2. Go to the Food Search screen
  3. Tap the barcode icon in the search bar
  4. Point your camera at one of these barcodes on your computer screen
  5. The app should automatically detect and search for the food

Manual Testing:

You can also manually test the API endpoints:

  • Working barcode:
    curl "http://localhost:8000/foods/barcode/012345678905"
  • Invalid barcode:
    curl "http://localhost:8000/foods/barcode/999999999999"
